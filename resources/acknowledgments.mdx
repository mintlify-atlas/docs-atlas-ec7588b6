## LliureX Team

A special acknowledgment to the **LliureX Team** at the Conselleria de Educación (Valencian Ministry of Education).

<Accordion title="About LliureX">

LliureX is an educational Linux distribution developed by the Valencian regional government for use in schools throughout the Valencian Community. It's based on Ubuntu and provides a complete educational environment with tools for teaching and learning.

The project represents one of the most successful educational technology initiatives in Spain, providing free and open-source software to thousands of students and educators.

</Accordion>

### Personal Reflection

Everything learned through contributions to the LliureX educational distribution formed the foundation of this course material. The experience of developing educational technology for the Conselleria de Educación was invaluable.

**To all my colleagues on the LliureX team...many thanks.**

## Contributing to the Community

This project embraces the principles of educational resource sharing:

<Accordion title="Open Educational Resources (OER) Values">

**Access**: Educational materials should be freely available to all learners and educators.

**Adaptation**: Resources should be modifiable to meet diverse educational contexts and needs.

**Collaboration**: Sharing improvements benefits the entire educational community.

**Quality**: Open peer review and iteration improve materials over time.

**Sustainability**: Free resources reduce barriers to education and enable long-term availability.

</Accordion>
